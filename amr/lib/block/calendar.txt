The role :calendar is used for the calendars of various cultures:
<ul>
   <li> Gregorian calendar (modern Western, de facto international standard)
   <li> Julian calendar (old Western, differs from Gregorian calendar in which years are leap <nobr>y</nobr>ears)
   <li> Islamic calendar (lunar calendar; 1 AH = 622 AD, 1434 AH = Nov. 14, 2012 to Nov. 4, 2013)
   <li> Hebrew calendar (lunisolar calendar = lunar calendar with leap months; 5773 AM = Sept. 16, 2012 - Sept. 4, 2013)
   <li> Japanese calendar (regnal solar calendar)
</ul>

The role :calendar is also used for <nobr>y</nobr>ears that differ from the standard calendar <nobr>y</nobr>ear.
In these cases, the concept year is included:
<ul>
   <li> academic years
   <li> fiscal years
</ul>
