TITLE :poss
HIGHLIGHT-RED :poss

The role :poss (&quot;possessed by&quot;) is a very general form of possession.
Often used with possessive pronouns, as well as <i>of</i> and <i>'s</i>.

EXAMPLE isi_0002.205 The boy lost his toy.

EXAMPLE isi_0002.38 Whose car is this ?

EXAMPLE isi_0002.368 But that's not his fault.

Reifications: own-01, have-03

<hr>

Do <b>not</b> use :poss in cases such as

EXAMPLE isi_0002.37 the roof of the house

EXAMPLE isi_0002.79 a ring of gold

EXAMPLE isi_0002.24 My cousin is a lawyer .
