Official timezones are annotated as strings:

EXAMPLE isi_0002.180 10:00 UTC

EXAMPLE isi_0002.7 Wednesday, February 29, 2012, at 4:30pm PST

Local and inofficial timezones are annotated using concepts:

EXAMPLE isi_0002.178 eight o'clock in the morning local time

EXAMPLE isi_0002.179 10am California time

<p>
See also general examples for <a href="http://www.isi.edu/~ulf/amr/lib/popup/date.html">date entities</a>.
