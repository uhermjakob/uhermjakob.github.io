:instrument describes the physical object used in an action, typically a tool, device, weapon, or a body part such as a finger or fist.
<br>
If you want to more generally describe how or in what way something is done, then consider using <a href="http://www.isi.edu/~ulf/amr/lib/popup/manner.html">:manner</a> instead.

EXAMPLE isi_0002.46 I ate pasta with a fork .

EXAMPLE isi_0002.332 Iraq launched a missile attack.

EXAMPLE isi_0002.338 He ate the pizza with his fingers.

EXAMPLE isi_0002.339 He recovered the frisbee with a long stick.

Make sure you use any core role :ARGx designated for instruments, if available:

EXAMPLE isi_0002.337 He caught the fish with a net.
