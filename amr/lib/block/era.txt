The role :era is used for calendar eras, including regnal eras.
<p>
Western calendar: BC, AD.
<p>
In the Japanese calendar, eras correspond to the reigns of Japanese emperors: Meiji (1868-1912), Taisho (1912-1926), Showa (1926-1989), Heisei (since 1989)

EXAMPLE isi_0002.167 Caesar was murdered in 44 BC .

EXAMPLE isi_0002.168 Three Roman legions were annihilated in the Battle of the Teutoburg Forest in 9 AD .

EXAMPLE isi_0002.181 Augustus lived from 63 BCE to 14 CE.

EXAMPLE isi_0002.169 The 9.3 Tohoku earthquake occured in the year Heisei 23 .

Note: The Japanese year Heisei 23 corresponds to the year 2011 in the Western calendar.
<p>
<b>DO NOT</b> use :era for geological eras (such as the Mesozoic Era) or colloquial eras (such as the Victorian Era).
