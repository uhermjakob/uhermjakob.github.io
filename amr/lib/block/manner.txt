TITLE :manner
HIGHLIGHT-RED :manner

:manner describes <b>how</b> something is done,
including methods to do something (or "means") and modes of transportation.
<br>
However, use the more specific roles
<a href="http://www.isi.edu/~ulf/amr/lib/popup/instrument.html">:instrument</a> or
<a href="http://www.isi.edu/~ulf/amr/lib/popup/medium.html">:medium</a> if any of those apply.

EXAMPLE isi_0001.25 The boy is a hard worker.

EXAMPLE isi_0002.331 He decorated the room in a creative way.

EXAMPLE isi_0002.340 The company obtained the permit through bribery.

EXAMPLE isi_0002.144 The mayor proposed to lower crime by hiring more police officers.

EXAMPLE isi_0002.333 Nicole went to England by train.

EXAMPLE isi_0002.323 Lindbergh crossed the Atlantic in the Spirit of St. Louis.
