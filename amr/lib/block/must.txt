The modal must is generally annotated as obligate-01, with a <i>:domain</i> complement:

EXAMPLE isi_0002.59 The boy must go.

EXAMPLE isi_0001.5 The boy must not go.

EXAMPLE isi_0002.60 The boy has to go.

<hr>

In some cases, however, it conveys an inference ("epistemic" must):

EXAMPLE isi_0002.218 John isn't here, he must be sick.
