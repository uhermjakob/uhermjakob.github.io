TITLE subset of superset
HIGHLIGHT-RED include-91

Constructions such as <font color="blue">10% of X</font>, which involve
a subset and a superset, are annotated using include-91.

EXAMPLE isi_0002.17 10% of smokers die of lung cancer.

<b>Shortcut:</b> Annotators may use pseudo-roles :subset/:subset-of as a shortcut for include-91.
