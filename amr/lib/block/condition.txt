The role :condition is used for if, unless, and in case of.

EXAMPLE isi_0002.81 The game will be canceled if it rains .

EXAMPLE isi_0002.82 In the case of rain, the game will be canceled . 

For negative conditionals such as unless, include :polarity -

EXAMPLE isi_0002.83 The game will be canceled unless the rain stops .
