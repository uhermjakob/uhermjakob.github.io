TITLE :domain
HIGHLIGHT-RED :domain

This role is used in constructions such as <i>... is &lt;noun&gt;</i> and <i>... is &lt;adjective&gt;</i>:

EXAMPLE isi_0002.24 My cousin is a lawyer.

EXAMPLE isi_0001.7 The girl is sad.

If there is a specific frame for an adjective, such as for <i>possible</i>, use that frame and its :ARGx instead.

The role :domain is also used under modal concepts such as <i>likely</i>:

EXAMPLE isi_0001.10 The boy is likely to go.
