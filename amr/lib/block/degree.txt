<h2>Overview</h2>
<ul> 
   <li> Use have-degree-91 (or have-quant-91) for comparatives and superlatives
      <ul>
	 <li> the most expensive dress
	 <li> the bright<font color="red">est</font> boy
      </ul>
   <li> Use have-degree-91 (or have-quant-91) for consequence constructions with too / enough / so
      <ul>
	 <li> It is too early to reach any conclusions.
	 <li> This computer is small enough to fit in your pocket.
	 <li> He was so tired that he slept for 10 hours.
      </ul>
   <li> Use :degree for intensifiers and downtoners.
      <ul>
	 <li> The boy sang very beautifully.
	 <li> He is kind of sad.
	 <li> Carthage was utterly destroyed.
      </ul>
   <li> Use :degree so for wh-exclamations.
      <ul>
	 <li> What a great book! &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; <font color="#777777">= so great a book</font>
	 <li> How beautiful you are. &nbsp; &nbsp; <font color="#777777">= You are so beautiful!</font>
      </ul>
</ul>
