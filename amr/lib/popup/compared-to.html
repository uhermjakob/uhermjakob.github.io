:compared-to   (AMR annotation guidance)

Obsolete
Formerly used in comparatives, superlatives. Replaced by have-degree-91 and have-quant-91 .

Example:  "the girl is taller than the boy"
     (h / have-degree-91
           :ARG1 (g / girl)
           :ARG2 (t / tall)
           :ARG3 (m / more)
           :ARG4 (b / boy))

Example:  "She is the tallest girl on the team ."
     (h / have-degree-91
           :ARG1 (s / she)
           :ARG2 (t / tall)
           :ARG3 (m / most)
           :ARG5 (g / girl
                 :ARG0-of (h2 / have-org-role-91
                       :ARG1 (t2 / team))))
