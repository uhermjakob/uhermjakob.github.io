:degree   (AMR annotation guidance)

Overview

  • Use have-degree-91 (or have-quant-91) for comparatives and superlatives
    • the most expensive dress
    • the brightest boy
  • Use have-degree-91 (or have-quant-91) for consequence constructions with too / enough / so
    • It is too early to reach any conclusions.
    • This computer is small enough to fit in your pocket.
    • He was so tired that he slept for 10 hours.
  • Use :degree for intensifiers and downtoners.
    • The boy sang very beautifully.
    • He is kind of sad.
    • Carthage was utterly destroyed.
  • Use :degree so for wh-exclamations.
    • What a great book!           = so great a book
    • How beautiful you are.     = You are so beautiful!
